Research News

Dr. Vinit’s new innovation on “3-D image quality assessment” can automatically judge the quality of a video without any prior information. This innovation can be utilised by video streaming companies or a security company.

The innovation by Dr. Tanveer can be directly used to predict the “human interest” of person for a task. Employers can use this to quantify the interest of their employees in assigned projects.

Dr. Deepak and Dr. Shivani demonstrated proof of concept of Mini-MOOCs with different demographic variance in India (1 million record analysed under this project till date).

Dr. Deepak’s work uses Machine Learning to estimate toxicity levels of different pharmaceutical products, many of which are not tested fully before their launch in the market.

Dr Vipul is working on the “Hidden Hunger” project to develop an automated system for flour fortification which will help reduce micronutrient deficiency.
